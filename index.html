Courses We Offer

Noorani Qaida

This is the first step when you start learning Quran. At the end of this comprehensive and customizable course, you or your kids will be able to read Quran by themselve. Our highly qualified tutors will start with you from the basics like learning the Arabic alphabet, reading simple words, then reading complete sentences, and eventually reading portions of Quran.

Namaz & Ethics

This course is considered as the most important and compulsory course for all the students. At the end of this course, your kids will be able to offer Namaz (Salah) by themselve. Our tutors will teach each & every thing from basics to advance about Namaz (e:g lesson, Rakats and steps, Faraiz, wajibat etc).   Besides Namaz, your kids will learn Ethics, Sunnah's, daily prayers (Dua's) and a couple of Ahadith in this course.

Quran Reading (NAZRA)

In this module, Our Quran teaching experts (males & females) will teach your kids all features of Quran recitation. At the end of this course, you or your kids will be able to recite Quran very fluently. You will learn the proper pronunciation of verses of Quran, different rules of reciting the Quran, and even the etiquette of Quran recitation.

Tajweed

It is considered as innovative step for mastering Quran recitation. Some people believe that studying with tajweed is not obligatory for accurate recitation of Quran, but, they are mistaken, It is.
Through this advanced and customizable online course, you or your kids will learn Quran's recitation with Tajweed from a professional mentor who master the recitation with Tajweed.

Memorization (HIFZ)

This course is specially designed for people that live in non-muslim countries like where they don't have proper setups for them or their kids to be a Hafiz/ Hafiza. This online course has 3 levels:
1. Memorization of 1 part (sipara)
2. Memorization of 5 parts (sipara)
3. Memorization of all 30 parts (sipara).
Through this particular and personalized course, you or your kids can learn memorizing Quran online from our expert Quran tutors who already are Hafiz/Hafiza.

Translation (TAFSEER)

In this module, you will learn the meaning of verses word by word, Islamic laws, quranic ideas (social, political and religious) through the oral display, comparison of Quran with other previously revealed scriptures, and much more. By end of this Tafsir program, you will be able to interpret any Quran verses properly with needful proofs. You will surely enjoy this course if you really want to understand what exactly Quran wants to talk with us about.
